NGX-ICONS

Are you tired of relying on the same old icons in your Angular projects? Look no further than NgxIcons! Our straightforward package allows you to easily use your own custom icons in your Angular applications.

In this demonstration, we are utilizing the meticulously crafted heroicons, created by the talented TailwindCSS team. However, the purpose of this package is to provide a straightforward tool for Angular that allows you to easily use your own custom icons. We've downloaded these icons and re-optimized it with the help of the @vonlof/generate-icons package.
